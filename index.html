I heard from a bird that the world lost another one
But this one was different
This was one you knew very well
This was one you loved, and one who loved you

The bird sang a short song about him:
He lived a long life
He lived a full life
He played (remember play? oh, how we forget to play!)
He learned
Worked
Fought
Loved
And lost
And he had kids!
And one of his kids had more kids!

I wonder if his kids ever thought about how

He saw the future
The future always comes in the form of a present
And so the cycle goes

I can’t help but think that in his final hours
He thought of you
And that your presence in this world
Brought him peace, satisfaction, and pride

And as he began to exit the stage
